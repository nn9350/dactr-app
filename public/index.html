A dentist named Dr. Nitin, who owns two dental clinics in Gwalior, Madhya Pradesh, built this. He's been a dentist for over 15 years and was frustrated that no good software existed for clinics like his.

  • Fewer people will need to travel to big cities just to get basic dental care
